## 🏢 Who We Are

**Sentradesk** is a UK-based software company delivering intelligent, AI-powered business management platforms for enterprises, service providers, ISPs, educational institutions, and growing businesses worldwide.

We build software that powers the backbone of day-to-day operations — from customer relationship management to network billing, from learning management systems to full-scale ERP solutions.
