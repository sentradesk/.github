## 🏢 About Sentradesk

> *"We provide AI-enabled ERP, LMS, CRM, ISP OSS/BSS, and operational management solutions for enterprises, service providers, ISPs, institutions, and growing businesses."*

**Sentradesk** is a UK-based technology company that builds intelligent, AI-powered software platforms to power the backbone of modern business operations. From ISPs managing subscriber billing, to institutions running learning programmes, to enterprises automating workflows — Sentradesk delivers robust, scalable solutions.

We also build premium **WordPress themes & plugins** trusted by millions of website owners worldwide.
